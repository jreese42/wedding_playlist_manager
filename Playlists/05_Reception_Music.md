# Reception Music (The Yacht Celebration)

**Duration:** 3.5 Hours
**Purpose:** Fostering conversation and happiness across generations.
**Vibe:** "Sophisticated Backyard Party" meets "Yacht Rock." Avoid too much heavy EDM or "line dance" songs, but some suggestions of wedding classics are acceptable.
**Decades to Hit:** 60s Soul, 70s Soft Rock, 80s Pop, 90s R&B, and Modern Americana. Upbeat celebratory modern pop and rock. 90's and 2000's throwback anthems for a firl growing up in the 90s. This is a long playlist, so there is room for tons of variety!

## Notes from James
- I think this playlist could use some extra 90's country to keep the vibe sup. Gretchen wilson and more.
